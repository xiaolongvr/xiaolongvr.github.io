Short Bio

I'm a Ph.D. student, supervised by Prof. Lili Wang, in the State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems, School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University. Before that, I received my Master's Degree from China Agricultural University in 2018, supervised by Prof. Huixin Zhou and Prof. Xiuying Tang.

Research interests

  • Redirected Locomotion in VR
  • 3D Interaction Techiniques
